EXERCISES

A series of fundamental exercises were structured by the 15th century Saint Purandara Dasa of Kshemarapura in Shivamogga, India. These basic lessons, called Svaravalis and Alankāras, are set to Raga Māyamālava Gowla.

Click on the exercises below to open them in the Phrasebook. Select each (by clicking on the gray bar) to play.

2. Janta Varisai (Twin sequences)
