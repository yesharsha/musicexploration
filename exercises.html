Exercises

Instructions

Step 1: Use the button to toggle the Shruti on and off. You should hear a drone in the background. The drone is not continuous as of now, please cooperate.

Step 2: Pick up a sequence of notes eg. S R2 G3 M1 P D2 N3 S' or S R2 G2 M1 P D2 N2 S'. Make sure you pick one from each swara!

Step 3: Play the following exercises using the on-screen keyboard. These are a bunch of 8x8 rhythms. One note per beat.

Optional: Sing along with each swara. See footnote for tips on pronunciation.

Optional: Play the S, P and S' and spend time contemplating on these base notes.

1. Free flowing sequence

S R G M P D N S' |
S' N D P M G R S ||

S R G M S R G M |
S R G M P D N S' ||
S' N D P S' N D P |
S' N D P M G R S ||

S R G M P D N S' ||
S' N D P M P D N |
S' N D P M G R2 S ||

S R G M P R G M |
S R G M P D N S' ||
S' N D P M N D P |
S' N D P M G R2 S ||

S R G R G M P M |
S R G M P D N S' ||
S' N D N D P M P |
S' N D P M G R2 S ||

2. Twin sequence

SS RR GG MM PP DD NN S'S' |
S'S' NN DD PP MM GG RR SS ||

SS RS SR SR SS RR GG MM |
PP DP PD PD PP DD NN S'S' ||
S'S' NS' S'N S'N S'S' NN DD PP |
MM GM MG MG MM GG RR SS ||

SS RR GG RR SS RR GG MM |
PP DD NN DD PP DD NN S'S' ||
S'S' NN DD NN S'S' NN DD PP |
MM GG RR GG MM GG RR SS ||

SR GR RG MG SS RR GG MM |
PD ND DN S'N PP DD NN S'S' ||
S'N DN ND PD S'S' NN DD PP |
MG RG GR SR MM GG RR SS ||

Congratulations. You're now capable of permuting and combining such Swaras on your own.
