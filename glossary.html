Shruti

The basis of all Carnatic music is the Shruti which is a continuous drone that plays in the background. All notes are relative to this frequency which is set to the first piano key (Sa).

Swara

Swaras are notes as found in a piano. Eg. Sa, Ri, Ga, Ma

Ragam/Raga

Several notes are combined together in different ways to create different moods called Ragas.

Talam/Tala

Talam is a rhythm.

Arohanam

Arohanam is an ascending sequence of notes.

Avarohanam

Avarohanam is a descnding sequence of notes.

Moorchana

Moorchana is the collection of Arohanam and Avarohanam sequences.
