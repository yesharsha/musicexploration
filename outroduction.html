Outroduction

Scope

These set of interactive learnings will help you understand Ragas and carnatic music theory in a better perspective.

Depending on how much time you've spent here, you will have a better canvas to start with, when you are composing music on digital software.

You will have maybe discovered new Ragas/moods/colours.

I have done these learnings, what next?

Attend the next Carnatic music concert in your city.

Do not forget to research a little bit into the history and tradition of Carnatic Music.

Learn to sing some songs, just in case.

Accuracy

The database of Ragas has been scraped from this wikipedia link containing a list of 944 ragas.

Although it is mostly perfect, some mistakes might have crept in.

Contact me for any suggested changes and improvements in the software.
